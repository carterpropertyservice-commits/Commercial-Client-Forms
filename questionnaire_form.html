Client Discovery Questionnaire

Our path is guided by information.

OPENING:

"To match you with the right commercial space, I'd like to understand your operational needs and requirements."

Contact Information
A. Business Model
1. What transactions will you handle? Please describe your business model.
2. Do you expect high walk-in volume or mostly appointment-based customers?
3. How long have you been in business? Is this a new venture or a relocation?
B. Space Requirements
4. How much square footage do you need ideally? What's the minimum acceptable?
5. Do you need showroom space, private office, back room, or vault space? Please describe your floor plan needs.
6. Any electrical needs—dedicated circuits, high-intensity lighting, machinery, equipment?
C. Security
7. What are your non-negotiable security features—cameras, reinforced glass, controlled access, vault?
8. Do you need a discreet rear entrance for deliveries or armored delivery service?
9. What's the approximate inventory value you'll have on premises?
D. Location
10. Do you prefer high-visibility or a more discreet location? Why?
11. How important is close parking or rear access to your operations?
12. Are there specific areas or submarkets you're targeting?
E. Budget
13. What's your comfort range on monthly rent? Do you have a CAM ceiling?
14. Are you looking for a short-term lease or longer-term stability?
F. Licensing
15. Will permitting be an issue that may impact your ability to enter a contract? (What permitting considerations are required?)
16. Are there any regulatory considerations regarding areas, buildings, or terms? (Any known limitations.)
Timeline & Follow-Up
What's your timeline for finding a space?
Best way to reach you?
Anything else you'd like me to know?
CLOSING:

"Thank you for this information. I'll narrow down options that meet your security, zoning, CAM, and layout requirements. I'll put together a short list and schedule tours for the best matches."
